Traditional Taxi

The most traditional tranportation method is the taxi. Ho Chi Minh has Mai Linh taxi and Vinasun taxi which are the oldest in Vietnam. These taxi company have proven their professionalism and reliability over the years.

However, nowadays, because of the investment of foreign companies, the emergence of transportation companies has attracted a lot of Vietnamese. In addition, the average price of tradition taxi is more expensive than service taxi. Thus, tradditional taxi seems to be inferior to service taxi like Grab, Be, Gojek, and etc.

Mai Linh taxi hotline: (028) 38 27 79 79

Vinasun taxi hotline: (028) 38 27 27 27

Service Taxi

Grab, Be, and Gojek are the most common service taxi in Ho Chi Minh city. It had been familiar with Vietnamese the past few years.

Grab, which started the trend, is the first brand that came to Vietnam. Following by Be, and Gojek. These taxi are flexible in pricing, but overall, it is cheaper than the traditional taxi. You can book a car by using their brand app.
